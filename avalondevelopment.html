Q: What changes are you planning to make for early access?
A: I would like to make the following changes
  • Giving every starting faction a unique mission tree and mechanic or ship line
  • Making the game more interesting and engaging. Improving things like graphics and AI.
  • fixing bugs (obviously)
  • Adding custom scenario editor
  • Probably getting rid of the current missions systems and replacing the Victory condition with something more interesting and substatial than just "highest points wins"
Q: How long will you work on this game?
A: Over the course of a year or so from Early Access release. I will work on it regardless of interest
Q: Why does this game have such a small internet presence
A: I am a hobbyist doing this for fun
