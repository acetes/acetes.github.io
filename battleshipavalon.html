This is the site for the game Battleship Avalon. Avalon is a strategy game combining grand strategy with 4x and RTS elements. It is based around the design of old strategy games, with a shorter sandbox-like campaign that allows you to experiment, and a small number of core mechanics which interact in emergent ways to produce variety in playstyles. It's currently in a very early state, but I have lots of plans.
