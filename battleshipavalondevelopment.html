Q: What changes are you planning to make for early access?

  • Making the game more interesting and engaging. Improving things like graphics and AI.
  • fixing bugs (obviously)
  • Adding modding/custom scenario editor and localization support making it very easy to do overhaul mods for different settings, improving accessibility
